3. Educational Tracks in Phase 3
Phase 3 centers on four structured learning tracks:
3.1 Track A — Qaida Baghdadi Mastery (Foundational Curriculum)
This is the starting point for 99% of children worldwide.
Curriculum Stages
1.	Arabic Alphabet Recognition
o	Visual demonstration of letters
o	Mouth positioning (Makharij)
o	Student repeats → AI evaluates sound
2.	Vowels & Harakat
o	Fatha, Kasra, Dhamma
o	Madd (long vowels)
o	Tanween
o	Sukoon
3.	Joining Letters
o	Beginning, middle, end forms
o	Word construction practice
4.	Basic Quranic Words
o	Short words directly from real Quranic text
5.	Sentence Building
o	Simple ayat structured into reading practice
6.	Intro to Tajwīd
o	Ghunnah
o	Qalqalah
o	Idgham
o	Heavy/light letters
AI Teacher Behaviors
•	Models pronunciation
•	Provides animated mouth‑shape hints
•	Evaluates child’s recitation accuracy
•	Encourages with praise: “Excellent! One more time!”
•	Unlocks levels as mastery increases
Content Requirements
•	Custom-designed curriculum (proprietary IP)
•	Audio assets for each letter and example word
•	Whisper‑based recognition tuned for child pronunciations
